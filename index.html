Revoking Permissions

We are working on a revoke tool that better supports Telos.

In the meantime if you would like to revoke permissions for an application in MetaMask, follow these steps:

  1. Click the kebab menu (three dots) next to your username.
  2. Select 'connected sites'.
  3. Click 'disconnect' for each app you wish to revoke permissions.

Keep in mind that unlike a revoke tool, this will revoke all permissions. Once the tool is live, you'll be able to revoke only those permissions which grant direct access to your assets.
